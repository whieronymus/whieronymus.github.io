Draft

  • Format: Snake draft (randomized order). Consider Third‑Round Reversal in 12‑team leagues.
  • Clock: 60–90s per pick in live draft.
  • Keepers: Off by default. Commissioners may propose keeper rules pre‑season.
  • Dynasty/Taxi: Not applicable for redraft. If enabled, document separately.

Waivers & Transactions

  • Waiver Type: FAAB (Free Agent Acquisition Budget)
  • Budget: $200 (virtual). Bids are blind; highest bid wins. $0 bids allowed.
  • Processing: Primary run: Wednesday 10:00 AM. Secondary runs daily at 10:00 AM for dropped players.
  • Add/Drop: Continuous waivers during the week; post‑waiver players become free agents until game lock.
  • Trades: Deadline at NFL Week 11 end. Review by commissioner; veto for collusion only.
  • IR: Disabled for the 2025–2026 season.

Playoffs

  • Teams: 6 of 12 qualify (Seeds 1–6)
  • Byes: Top 2 seeds receive first‑round byes
  • Weeks: NFL Weeks 15–17
  • Seeding: Record → Points For → Head‑to‑Head
  • Toilet Bowl: Optional consolation bracket for non‑playoff teams
  • Scheduling: No Week 18 matchups (rest risks)

League Policies

  • Sportsmanship: Trash talk encouraged within reason; hate speech and personal attacks are not tolerated.
  • Lineup Deadlines: Player spots lock at individual game kickoff.
  • Inactive Managers: Commissioner may set a fair lineup if two consecutive weeks are neglected.
  • Rule Changes: Off‑season voting, simple majority unless otherwise stated. In‑season changes require 2/3 and cannot affect prior results.
  • Money: Any buy‑ins/payouts handled off‑platform between members.
  • Ties: Regular season ties stand. Playoff ties broken by bench points, then higher seed.

Optional Variants (Commissioner Picks)

Superflex

Replace one FLEX with a Superflex (QB/RB/WR/TE) to increase QB value and draft diversity.

TE Premium

Set TE receptions = +1.5 (others remain at +1.0) to elevate mid‑tier TEs.

No K / No DEF

Remove both and add +1 FLEX (or two) for more skill‑position strategy.

Bonuses

  • 100+ rush/rec yards: +3
  • 300+ pass yards: +3
  • 40+ yard TD: +1

Third‑Round Reversal

For 12+ teams, consider TRR to balance early draft position edge.
